Sterre Witlox

1st year MSc Integrated Product Design student @Delft University of Technology, drawing visualisation teaching assistant and freelance designer.

As a student of the TU Delft I participated in an interdisiplinary project in collaboration with Royal Delft to create Delft Blue painted using a Universal Robots UR5 robot. Aside from this we also modified a 3D printer to prototype with parallel to the robot. The aim of this project was to innovate the traditional Delft Blue to prevent it from going out of style. The duration of this project was 10 weeks at full time participation In this project my main function was the illustrative design for the tiles using Adobe Illustrator, making artistic choices as well as general project organisation and management.
